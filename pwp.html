Personal Website

Purpose

The purpose of creating this website is to make a professional portfolio focused on front-end and back-end web design

Audience

The audience of this web-page are going to be primarily professional individuals, searching for people looking into web development and I.T. related positions. other people i am targeting as an audience are small business owners with a growing business, company employers with a high reputation in the tech industry, and simply, my own peers.

Goal

The goal of this website is to give employers a way to view my professional profile, and to contact me if they decide to present me with an open position. A secondary goal of this website is to receive offers or recommendations to other opportunities that allow me to further my education in web design and security.
